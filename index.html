Sustainability

As well as being a web developer, I am highly passionate about taking action to tackle climate change. I am a Director and part-time employee of York Community Energy and also a qualified Carbon Literacy Trainer working with Speak Carbon Collective.

Naturally, I care about the climate impacts of everything I do, including web development. I take a minimalistic design approach and optimise my code as much as possible to reduce the amount of computing power required to run my websites. This translates into reduced energy usage and thus lower emissions. So if you want a website with great green credentials, I'm your dev!
